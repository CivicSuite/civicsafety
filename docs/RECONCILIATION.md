# CivicSafety Reconciliation

The canonical catalog defines CivicSafety as non-CJIS public-safety administrative support: policy and SOP Q&A, training record tracking, PIO drafting support, non-CJI records-request assistance, body-worn-camera retention metadata without video AI, and aggregate public crime-statistics publishing.

v0.1.0 intentionally ships deterministic foundations only. It does not ingest CJI, connect to CAD/RMS, support dispatch, investigate cases, drive enforcement, manage evidence, provide legal advice, call live LLMs, or ship public-safety connector runtime.
